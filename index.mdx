FemeSalud is a premium clinical management suite designed for gynecology and obstetrics professionals. Built on TanStack Start, React 19, Supabase, and Tailwind CSS, it delivers real-time appointment scheduling, structured electronic medical records, integrated billing, and branded PDF document generation — all with server-side rendering for fast load times.
